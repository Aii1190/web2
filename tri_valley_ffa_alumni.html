About Tri‑Valley FFA Alumni

Tri‑Valley FFA Alumni supports the Tri‑Valley FFA chapter by funding scholarships, sponsoring events, and offering mentorship to students. We're a group of community members, parents, and alumni committed to the success of our youth.

  • • Fundraise for scholarships and travel
  • • Host career development and community events
  • • Volunteer mentorship and program support
